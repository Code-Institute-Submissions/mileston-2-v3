How To Play

  1. Click "Play Now" in the navigation bar
  2. Once on game page the game will start imediately
  3. Click your mouse button or touch your screen to make your dragon fly!
  4. Try to guide your dragon between the water pipes!
  5. And thats it! Try not to get too triggered ;)
